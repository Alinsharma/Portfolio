About Me

Hi! I’m Alin Sharma, a passionate and enthusiastic Computer Science undergraduate with a deep interest in C, C++, and modern web development. Over time, I've evolved from learning basic syntax to building real-world web projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS — and I’m constantly upgrading my skill set.

I’ve gained diverse experiences beyond the classroom, such as working on RTO vehicle data entry systems, contributing to the Rajasthan Agriculture Fest (RAF)—a unique auction event showcasing innovation—and proudly participating in the G-20 Summit 2024 in Delhi. These opportunities have shaped my communication, adaptability, and teamwork.

In 2024, I successfully completed a Live Project Based Internship cum Training in C & C++ Programming from Samyak IT Solutions Pvt Ltd, an NSDC-authorized institute. Over the course of this program, held from August 24 to November 5, I worked on hands-on projects, strengthened my foundational programming knowledge, and earned an A+ grade. This experience helped sharpen my logic-building, problem-solving, and code optimization skills in both C and C++, reinforcing my passion for software development.

Whether technical or non-technical, I believe every experience teaches something valuable. My goal is to be part of a mission-driven team where I can leverage my skills to make scalable, positive impact.

In March 2024, I secured the 3rd position in the Startup Expo held during Pravah 2024 at Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management & Gramothan (SKIT). This recognition was awarded for demonstrating innovation, execution, and presentation skills in a competitive environment. The experience not only strengthened my entrepreneurial mindset but also refined my ability to work under pressure, communicate effectively, and turn ideas into impact.
